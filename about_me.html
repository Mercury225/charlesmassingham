Website Architect

A website architect is someone who builds websites and gives life to websites by communicating excellent design, as well as the organisation's values, brand-image and language. In the past i have spent time getting to know the people of an organisation, and manifesting their values through the use of art and design within practical considerations.

What got me into website development

I just recently started out in front-end development using HTML, CSS and Javascript to create my own websites and designs. I love discovering all the nuances that go into website creation, and admiring the final product once it's finished. The best part of this job is that something remains and is left for others to use and benefit from. So, i'm a website builder at heart. I hope to expand into more product-based tech like phone and gaming apps.
